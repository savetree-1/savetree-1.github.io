Weekly Reflections

Here are my key takeaways and learnings from each week of the PDP course.

Week 1: Career Development & Self-Awareness

Focused on self-awareness, strategic career planning, and building a portfolio mindset.

Key Learnings:

  • Gained clarity on personal strengths and long-term career goals.
  • Started aligning B.Tech journey with career aspirations.
  • Learned the importance of a portfolio to showcase skills and projects effectively.

Reflection: "Knowing myself is the first step toward making confident career choices."

Week 2: SWOC Analysis

Conducted a detailed self-evaluation to identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and challenges.

Key Takeaways:

  • Strengths: Unique skillsets and adaptability.
  • Weaknesses: Areas of improvement, like time management.
  • Opportunities: Training, hackathons, and networking events.
  • Challenges: Competition and resource constraints.

Reflection: "This week helped me see myself more clearly and plan my growth effectively."

Week 3: Johari Window & Self-Discovery

Explored unknown strengths and gaps through feedback and self-reflection techniques.

Learned the importance of collaboration, feedback, and adaptability in personal development.

Week 4: Glimpses

Mastered the VAK learning model and techniques for delivering effective presentations.

Key Learnings:

  • Tailored presentations for visual, auditory, and kinesthetic learners.
  • Practiced confident delivery using tone, eye contact, and time management.
  • Structured content logically and highlighted key takeaways.

Week 5: Verbal & Non-Verbal Communication

Learned how tone, gestures, and expressions enhance the communication process.

Practiced expressing ideas with clarity and alignment between verbal and non-verbal cues.

Week 6: Listening & Feedback

Mastered active listening and learned to provide constructive feedback effectively.

This week emphasized reducing assumptions and staying present in conversations.

Week 7: Group Discussion Skills

Developed confidence in structured debates and collaborative discussions.

Learned turn-taking, summarization, and respecting diverse viewpoints to enhance teamwork.

Week 8: Writing Effectively

Refined professional writing skills for clarity, brevity, and logical structure.

Focused on organizing ideas and improving the quality of emails, reports, and documents.

Week 9: Advanced Public Speaking

Enhanced public speaking skills using storytelling and quick-thinking frameworks.

Practiced delivering concise and engaging speeches with confidence and clarity.

Week 10: Public Speaking Mastery

Reflected on the journey and showcased learnings through structured presentations.

Key Learnings:

  • Set a clear purpose and structured talks effectively.
  • Engaged the audience using personal stories and the 3Vs (Voice, Verbal, Visual).
  • Overcame stage fright and improved delivery through practice.
